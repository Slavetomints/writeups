I've seen this before, this looks like the `Dancing Man cipher`, which first debuted in Sir Arthur Conan Doyle's "The Adventure of the Dancing Men". It is a simple substitution cipher that has a different position of the characters arms and legs for different letters and numbers, the key is as follows: ![dancing men cipher](https://www.geocachingtoolbox.com/pages/codeTables/dancingMen.png) Going back through each frame, we'll end up with `CIPHERED` and `DANCE`, which makes our flag!
