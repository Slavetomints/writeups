# Socials Challenge description: > Have a look around our socials, maybe you'll find some flags! Don't forget to hit follow while you're there > > Flag format: INTIGRITI{Twitter+YouTube+Reddit} > > https://linktr.ee/hackwithintigriti Again, another warmup question, they'll have to check out their twitter youtube and reddit to get the pieces of the flag that you need. After that you'll need to decode them to spell out your message. INTIGRITI{0110100000110000011100000011001101011111011110010011000001110101+5f336e6a30795f+ZDRfYzdm} The first part is obviously `binary`, then `hexadecimal`, then `base64`. A quick run through of `CyberChef` should sort things out. FLAG: `INTIGRITI{h0p3_y0u_3nj0y_d4_c7f}`
